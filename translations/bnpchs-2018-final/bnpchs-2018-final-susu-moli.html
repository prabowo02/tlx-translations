Description

Jelix likes the newly popular street food called Es Kepal Moli a lot. After receiving Jelix's Es Kepal Moli, one of his friends, Fingga got curious about the method of making Es Kepal Moli. Surprisingly, one of the ingredients used by Jelix is Moli branded milk.

Currently, there are $$$N$$$ bottles of Moli Milk in Jelix's fridge. Everyday, Jelix will use $$$A$$$ Moli Milk bottles to make Es Kepal Moli. To keep in stock, Jelix also buys $$$B$$$ Moli Milk bottles every day and then stores them in the fridge. Obviously, Jelix buys more Moli Milk bottles than the amount he uses each day. Fingga is curious about how many bottles of Moli Milks there are in Jelix's fridge after $$$K$$$ days. Help Fingga resolve it!

Input Format

Input starts with a single integer $$$T (1 \leq T \leq 100)$$$ the number of test cases. Each case contains 4 integers $$$N$$$, $$$A$$$, $$$B$$$, and $$$K$$$ $$$(1 \leq A < B, N \leq 100; 1 \leq K \leq 100)$$$.

Output Format

For each test case, output "Kasus #$$$X$$$: $$$Y$$$", where $$$X$$$ is the case number (starting from 1), and $$$Y$$$ is the number of milk bottles in the fridge after $$$K$$$ days.

Sample Input

3
5 2 4 1
7 3 6 2
10 2 8 3

Sample Output

Kasus #1: 7
Kasus #2: 13
Kasus #3: 28
