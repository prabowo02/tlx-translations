Description

Three weeks have passed since Jeli successfully challenged Feli to study. Feli, who has been continuously studying for the last 3 weeks, now challenges Jeli to answer the same question.

For those of you who have forgotten about Bosu, Bosu is a rhythm game. In Bosu, triangles appear, and they have to be clicked in time. For every successful triangle click, your combo will be increased by 1 and your score will be increased by your combo amount. For example, your score is currently 10 and your combo is currently 4. If you then successfully clicked a triangle, your combo will be 5, and your score will be 15. At the start of the game, both your combo and score are 0.

"Three weeks ago, you successfully determined my final score in Bosu by just the amount of triangles in the game.", Feli said to Jeli.

"Yes, and that made you study, didn't it?", Jeli replied.

"Now, I will challenge you again with the same question!", Feli challenged.

"What is my final score if there are $$$N$$$ triangles and I successfully clicked all of them in time?", Feli continues.

Jeli is surprised and immediately requests for your assistance. Help Jeli answer the question!

Input Format

Input starts with an integer $$$T$$$ — the number of test cases. The next $$$T$$$ lines describe a test case.

Each test case is an integer $$$N$$$$ — the amount of triangles given by Feli.

Output Format

For each test case, output "Case #$$$X$$$: $$$Y$$$", where $$$X$$$ is the case number (starting from $$$1$$$), and $$$Y$$$ is the answer of Feli's question.

Constraints

  • $$$1 ≤ T ≤ 1000$$$.
  • $$$1 ≤ N ≤ 4 × 109$$$.

Sample Input

2
4
1

Sample Output

Case #1 : 10
Case #2 : 1
